One Of My Story

When I was in middle school, I have some best friends. We alwasys
go out on friday night and have fun together during the weekend.
As time goes by, we go to high school but we are not in the same high school.
The connection between us is getting less and less.
Druing the high school, we can meet each other once in a few months.
But I never think that the brotherhood between us are decreased.
Now, I study in the United States, all of my best friends are in China.
We can hardly meet maybe 1 or 2 years. But everytime I go back to my hometown,
we always hungout just like what we do in the middle school. I really miss my best friedns.

Next year, one of my best firend is going to get married with his lover, we really feel happy for him.
I told them this winter holiday that I will come to the wedding ceremony no matter how busy I am.
It seems all of my best friends have their girlfriends, I really feel happy for them.
And also, I think I need to work hard to find my girlfriend. LOL.
